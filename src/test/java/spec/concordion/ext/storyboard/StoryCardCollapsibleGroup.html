Collapsible Group Card

Sometimes your test might perform a large number of steps and the storyboard becomes unweildy. Adding cards to a collapsible group will allow you to click on the first card to show or hide the rest of the cards in the group.

Behaviour:

  • A collapsible group card may be added explicitly by calling the startCollapsibleGroup(), stopCollapsibleGroup() or addCard(...) methods
  • Clicking on the collapsible group card image will show or hide the rest of the cards in the group
  • If an error occurs within the group the group is left expanded, otherwise it is rendered collapsed

Example 1: Add Card

<span concordion:execute="addGroup(#TEXT)">Setup</span>

When this snippet is run with this fixture, this results in a collapsible start card being added to the Storyboard.
